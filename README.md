IRSON - International Registry of Sports Organizations and Events, is a relational database that aims to keep records of sports organizations and their mutual memberships at the (inter)national level, history of sports teams for participation in leagues as well as mutual duels in any competition (leagues, cups and friendly matches) and history of athletes. The system also provides sophisticated business logic, i.e. offers transparency for the profit of a particular team in the last month, a dynamic view that returns a table for a particular season up to a certain time period and regulates the signing of contracts that would not be valid and similar restrictions when adding new records.
